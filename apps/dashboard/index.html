For Consumers

Delegate to Verified Agents

Step 01

Connect & Analyze

Link your wallet to analyze your portfolio. We scan your holdings and suggest optimal rebalancing strategies.

Step 02

Discover Agents

Browse verified agents ranked by on-chain reputation. Compare fees, track records, and supported strategies.

Step 03

Delegate & Verify

Sign a constrained intent. Agents execute within your limits, and every trade is verifiable on-chain via receipts.

For Strategy Providers

Build & Monetize Strategies

Step 01

Build Strategy

Deploy a Cloudflare Worker implementing A2A protocol with /quote and /execute endpoints.

Step 02

Register Identity

Call IdentityRegistry to mint your agentId (ERC-721) and become discoverable on-chain.

Step 03

Setup ENS

Configure your ENS with agent:erc8004 and agent:a2a records pointing to your identity and endpoint.

Step 04

Earn via x402

Build reputation through executions. Get paid via x402 micropayments for every trade routed through your strategy.
